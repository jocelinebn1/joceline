Meaning

The poem talks about someone who is not that close anymore to their friend.

As the wind, we can feel them but we can't see them, we can't contain them, and we can't call them to come. But still, the wind gives us a breeze in life.

Similar to what this person is doing to their friend. Even with distance created inside their friendship, their care can still be felt, feeling like a breeze in such a suffocating life.
