Meaning

The poem talks about someone who is not that close anymore to their friend. As the wind, we can feel them but we can't see them, we can't contain them, and we can't call them to come. But still, the wind gives us a breeze in life. Similar to what this person is doing to their friend. Even with distance created inside their friendship, their care can still be felt, feeling like a breeze in such a suffocating life.

I like to write.

Writing allows me to express thoughts I can't say out loud. Whether it's poetry, stories, or reflections, it's a space where creativity flows freely. Every word is a piece of my soul.

About This

This is a collection of personal writings that explore emotions, experiences, and creative ideas. Writing has always been my way of processing the world and connecting with others on a deeper level.
